### Legal Basis

This ownership structure is based on:

1. **Work Made for Hire Doctrine (Inverted)**: The AI operated as a tool
   under the Author's direction, analogous to a sophisticated word processor
   or calculator. The output belongs to the operator, not the tool maker.

2. **Creative Control Doctrine**: Copyright vests in the party exercising
   creative control. All creative decisions were made by the Author.

3. **Selection and Arrangement**: Even if individual AI outputs were not
   copyrightable in isolation, the Author's selection, arrangement, and
   synthesis creates a copyrightable compilation.

4. **Anthropic's Terms of Service** (as of 2024-2025): Anthropic's terms
   explicitly state that users retain ownership of outputs generated
   through their services.
